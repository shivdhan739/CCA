About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Students to be the creator and innovator.
Technology is been evolving and getting at the next level and there is no
proper reach to learn it. So we are here with passion of educating Students
in the easiest and interesting way in various field.

Educating someone is not just about teaching them something, it’s about
providing them the right path in particular field and moulding them in a
perfect shape. It’s also about inspiring them to enjoy the Learning by
making it in simpler way, through practical examples, building real world
live projects.

And this Journey of Passion started in 2019 “Is there any way that Students
learn the latest things about Coding and Designing in the most easiest
and interesting way and in the affordable price” and Coding Circle
Academy was born.
